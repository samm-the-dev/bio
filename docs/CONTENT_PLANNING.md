## Content Writing Tips

**Voice & Tone:**
- Conversational and authentic
- Avoid excessive formality
- Let personality show
- Be specific over generic

**Technical Descriptions:**
- Clear but not overly jargon-heavy
- Explain the "why" not just the "what"
- Include enough detail for understanding without overwhelming

**Keep It Real:**
- No marketing speak
- No inflated claims
- Honest about current status and goals
- Comfortable with work-in-progress

**Length Guidelines:**
- Home hero: 2-3 sentences
- Project descriptions: 1-2 short paragraphs
- About sections: 1-3 paragraphs each
- Now page: Bullet points or brief paragraphs, keep it scannable
