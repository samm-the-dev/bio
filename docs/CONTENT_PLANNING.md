## Content Voice Guidelines

**Voice & Tone:**

- Conversational and authentic
- Avoid excessive formality
- Let personality show
- Be specific over generic

**Technical Descriptions:**

- Clear but not overly jargon-heavy
- Explain the "why" not just the "what"

**Keep It Real:**

- No marketing speak or inflated claims
- Honest about current status and goals
- Comfortable with work-in-progress
